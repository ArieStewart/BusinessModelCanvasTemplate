The Business Model Canvas: The Calculator

By Ariel Stewart

Key Partners

  • SurgeWP
  • WordPress

Key Activities

Maintain an easy to use user interface to creating a custom development suite. While keeping customers informed on what impact their choice will have on their business structure.

Value Proposition

  • Giving our clients an easy to use visual interface to create a custom quote for a development suite.
  • Giving our clients the ability to track the set-up and availability of their suite

Customer Relationship

  • Integration with design companies to create continued business.
  • Added communication of tracking suite build to customers

Customer Segments

  • Design Companies who are looking to offer customers a quote for development suite, not just site design
  • Independent Developers looking for a full development suite

Key Resources

  • Pricing model
  • Hosting

Channels

  • Outreach to local design companies to create trial integration into their quoting process
  • Website to attract Independent Developers

Cost Structure

  • Server and Storage Costs
  • Web hosting costs

Revenue Streams

  • Subscription or licensing opportunities for design companies
  • On demand quoting for Independent Developers or smaller design firms.
